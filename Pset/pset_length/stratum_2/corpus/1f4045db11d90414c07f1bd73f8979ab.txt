# Web Hosting

#### Local website hosting with personalized support

Call us anytime you need us. We're right down the street in Morehead City, not
on the other side of the globe.

We don't cram thousands of websites on one server like the big guys. We take
personal care and responsibility for each and every website we host. Host your
site with the local pros that care about you. We'll move your site for you!

**Email Hosting:** Get a _@yourcompany.com_ email address! Our email servers
are secure and fast. Connect with your iPhone, iPad, Android, desktop
computer, or just have it forward mail to your existing email account! Let us
how how you want your email set up.

**Ultra Secure:** Security is essential to the integrity of your data. Our
servers are held to the strictest security standards. Every website we host is
encrypted with an SSL certificate. Ever noticed the green lock __icon at the
top of your web browser? That means the site is encrypted and secure. We
install SSL encryption for free for our clients because we take your security
seriously.

**Nightly Backups:** Your website, data, and email is backed up every single
night to two different secure servers on opposite ends of the continent. We
have disaster recovery plans in place so if something happens, we have you
covered.

**Proactive Management:** Ask us about our website management plans. In
addition to hosting your website, we can also provide on-going proactive
maintenance and support for your website. Website's do not take care of
themselves and they should not be neglected.
