Pompes à chaleur et systèmes de conditionnement d'air résidentiels : normes, essais et procédures américains.

US standards, testing and labelling procedures for residential heat pumps and air conditioners.

Auteurs : TOMLINSON J. J.

Type d'article : Article

Résumé

The system used in the US to arrive at energy performance standards for consumer products is based on the consensus of all interested parties. Overall, this system works effectively, as witnessed by the recent increase of about 10-20% in the minimum energy efficiency standards for air conditioners and heat pumps. Transparent labelling guidelines are also essential in providing consumers with adequate information.
