Beachfront Vacation Rental on Bald Head Island


Atlantic Bliss Bald Head Island is a wonderful oceanfront vacation rental house that will have you addicted to beach life. The location is fabulous and could only be better if you were sleeping on the beach. Why rough it when you can have it all – a great home and a private, direct beach access - with views for miles? If you’re not frolicking on the beach, you might be lounging on the screened porch or under the shady deck watching the tide roll in. This home is shining as bright as the sun. Three bedrooms on the main level have their own bathrooms and there’s a loft with twin beds that's perfect for the kids. 


Atlantic Bliss is located at the end of a quiet cul-de-sac, perfect for those with small children who like to ride bikes. A 4 and 6 seater golf cart are available for your use. BHI Club Guest membership (with golf privileges) is available for an additional fee paid directly to the club. Rents Sunday to Sunday in the summer.

Bald Head Island House Rentals

Bald Head Island offers a unique island escape conveniently located on the North Carolina coastline.  With no cars, quiet beaches, diverse nature and wildlife together with a rich history including NC's oldest lighthouse truly make this place special. Simply park your car at Deep Point Marina and catch a short ride on the passenger ferry to get to the island.  A pre-booked tram service is available on the island for arrivals and departures. Explore the island from our great location by golf cart, bicycle or on foot.  Atlantic Bliss is undoubtedly one of the best Beachfront Vacation Rentals on Bald Head Island. Once you discover this remarkable place you'll want to return!
