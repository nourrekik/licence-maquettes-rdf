# Welcome Summer Party with Oh Joy for Target Collection

October 9, 2014June 19, 2014 by
[Tammy](https://pinkpeppermintdesign.com/author/tammy/ "View all posts by

Hooray for Summer!! I don't know about you, but I would rather have my kids
home all the time and no schedule than them gone all day and have a strict
schedule. (probably why I'm a homeschooler! ðŸ˜‰ ) Maybe I'm funny like thatâ€¦but
I LOVE the freedom of summer!! ðŸ™‚ We've been enjoying summer for a few weeks
already, but all our friends are just celebrating their end of school days
this week and next, so I decided to set up a little welcome summer party! I
had been looking for any excuse to use this adorable "Oh Joy for Target"
summer line, and this was just the perfect reason. ðŸ™‚

We had picnic food (sandwiches, chips and cookies) in these adorable plastic
picnic trays. I wrapped the sandwiches in white deli wrap that you can at
Smart and Final, and added a few strips of washi tape to dress them up. ðŸ™‚

Fruit salad is colorful on it's own, but in these cute paper ice cream cups it
looked even more fun! I wanted to glam up the silverware just a bit, so I hit
the handles of it with a little bit of gold spray paint.

[![oh joy summer line target style party styling summer party ideas kids party

The super bright cups with gold rims were perfect for cold French Sodas from
World Market and I topped them with a simple pineapple star. This cute and
super easy touch takes the drinks up a notch and really makes them fit for a
party.

[![oh joy summer party shoot summer dinner party ideas birthday party ideas

Hot pink flowers, gorgeous green pitcher, and hot pink frame from HomeGoods
with our very own Hello Summer print inside were the perfect additions to the
adorable fruit print tray from Oh Joy.

[![oh joy summer shoot colorful party rainbow party summer
party](http://pinkpeppermintprints.com/pink/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/oh-joy-

I enlisted the help of my local grocery store bakery to whip up this cute
polka dot cake. I ordered a simple white cake with white buttercream frosting
and then added candy melt polka dots in colors to match the party.

You know the favors are my favorite part! ðŸ™‚ I wanted to show you all a clever

cut them up to make bags. I wanted to work with the fruit theme and found some
cute fruit candy that I could fill the bags with. The girls loved it!
